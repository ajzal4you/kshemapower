About Our Company

CORPORATE OVERVIEW

Kshema Power & Infrastructure Company Private Limited (KPICPL) founded by Dr. Satheesh Basant created success stories in infrastructure development for the power sector, particularly for the wind and solar power. With more than two decades of experience in this field, we successfully executed several contracts on a turnkey basis with an aggregate value of approximately 1000 crores. The company developed wind power projects with a total generation capacity of 2500MW in the states of Kerala, Tamil Nadu and Madhya Pradesh. We also executed Solar power projects with a collective capacity of 90MW in the states of Madhya Pradesh, Tamil Nadu and Andhra Pradesh.

Kshema Power plans to scale up its current operations in Kerala, Tamil Nadu, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Karnataka and Andhra Pradesh in association with several Independent Power Producers and High Net worth Individual clients. Kshema Power firmly holds a place in the mainstream renewable energy sector as an infrastructure and project management service provider. Today, Kshema as an enterprise spearheads the field of renewable energy development in India.

We provided the necessary infrastructure facilities to Gamesa Wind Turbines Pvt. Ltd., for establishing their 300 MW plant at Kayathar & Amba, 95 MW plant at Theni, to NEG Micon India Private Ltd., for their 200 MW plant at Veeranam, 150 MW plant at Ayakudi and to Enercon India Ltd., for their 300 MW plant at Manur.
