THE PEOPLE BEHIND

Dr. S. BASANT

Chairman & Managing Director

With more than 20 years of experience in diverse business sectors, Dr. S. Basant has been the main axle of our establishment. With a passion for renewable energy and a phenomenal business intelligence in the same, he sighted opportunities in this emerging renewable energy sector way back in the year 1998. The company reached the prestigious milestone of 350 MW of wind project development works executed in a single year (2015-16).

Dr. S. Basant is currently the Chairman of UKF College of Engineering & Technology, which offers B.Tech & M.Tech Programs with a Student capacity of 1000 Students and 200 Staff Members.

AWARDS & RECOGNITIONS
  • On 23rd October 2016, Dr. S. Basant received the Best Renewable Energy Developer Award at Madhya Pradesh from Mr. Upendra Tripathi, Secretary, Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE) in the presence of Mr. Manu Shristava, Principal Secretary, Renewable Energy & Mr. Jha, Director-Projects, NTPC.
  • On 29th July 2016, he was conferred with BRAND ICON 2016 - SOCIAL ENTERPRISES by The Times of India, Kerala.
  • Recognizing his incredible work in the field of renewable energy, Colombo University conferred him with a Doctor of Industry in 2013.

Satheesh Basant

Founder & MD

  • Dr. S. Basant is a visionary and astute businessman with an experience of more than 20 years in diverse sectors across India.
  • He entered the Wind Energy business in early 2000 and spot the opportunity in the emerging renewable sector in India.

  • Dr. S Basant is the chairman of UKF College of Engineering & Technology with a Student capacity of 1000+ Students and 200+ Staff Members.
  • Kshema Power recruits' young and vibrant engineers from this institute every year.
