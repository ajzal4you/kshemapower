THE JOURNEY

  • 2500 MW Milestone 2016
    From 500 MW in 2009 to 2500 MW in 2016 showed an extraordinary growth within 7 years. Won the best Renewable Energy Developer Award for Madhya Pradesh from MPNRED.
  • Double the Quantum & ISO Certified 2015
    EPC Contracts signed for 819 MW and work commenced for the same.

    Certified by Intertek for Integrated Management System (IMS) during the year.
  • Foray into Solar EPC 2014
    First contract for 50 MW Solar EPC with SunEdison signed.
  • 500 MW Milestone 2009
    Developed 500 MW wind farm development for Vestas between 2005 and 2009. Founder's dream of establishing a wind farm in Kerala became a reality .
  • Growth Phase 2005
    Transformed into a wind farm development company by undertaking construction of foundations, Road works, Transmission line works, BOP, Permits and Approvals .
  • Turning Point 2003
    NEG Micon recognizes Kshema's zeal for wind power and development and gave an order of land procurement for 50 MW Project in 2003.
  • The Foundation 1998
    Established by Dr. Basant during 1998 with a vision of building wind farms in Kerala.

Kshema Power & Infrastructure diversifies its business portfolio into three main business verticals and contributes to our society by excelling in them. Your search for developing a quality renewable energy infrastructure ends here!
